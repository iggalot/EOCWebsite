Clary

HC WTCH HTCh VX Winthrop Clary, CD RAE HXAc dMsM HXBc HXBdMsM EAC EJC ECC ROMX

Border Collie

DOB: 3/23/2010

Awards: Clary has earned the “Triple Crown of Herding Championships” in AKC, ASCA and AHBA. She was the first Border Collie to earn AKC’s Herding Excellent B Course Sheep Master title, and has won many High in Trials herding sheep, ducks and cattle. A truly versatile dog, Clary has also earned the Border Collie Society of America Versatility Excellent award, and with 6 of her 11 total offspring having Excellent titles in herding and/or agility, she has the Register of Merit Excellent award for contributing to the genetic advancement of the Border Collie breed.

Boaz

WTCH VX Winthrop Boaz, CDX RAE RM HSBds HXAds NA NAJ TKA NADAC Novice Versatility OAC

Border Collie

DOB: 7/1/2013

Awards: Boaz has won the Australian Shepherd Club of America herding championship (WTCH) that requires advanced work on sheep, ducks and cattle. He is accumulating points and wins toward the AKC Herding Champion title. He is also progressing toward the AKC Rally Championship (RACH), earned his CDX and is training for Utility in AKC obedience. In agility, Bo has NA, NAJ and in NADAC agility he has completed Novice Versatility, earned the Open Agility Certificate and Elite Tunnelers titles. He has the Border Collie Society of America Versatility Excellent award. He also has the Trick Dog Advanced title. We keep very busy with all of his activities!
